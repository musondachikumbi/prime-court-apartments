Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Prime Court Apartments ("Prime Court," "we," "us," or "our") operates this website and provides apartment booking and related services in Mkushi, Zambia. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our website, make a booking, schedule an appointment, or interact with our AI Assistant.

By using this website or our services, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the website or submit your information to us.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Identity & contact information: full name, phone number, email address.
  • Booking information: apartment type, check-in/check-out dates, number of guests, special requests.
  • Appointment information: preferred viewing dates and times, apartment of interest.
  • Communication data: messages you send through our contact form, booking form, or AI Assistant chat.
  • Payment-related information: where applicable, limited billing details necessary to process payment for your stay. We do not store full card details on our own servers.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited, collected automatically through standard website logs and cookies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and confirm apartment bookings and viewing appointments.
  • Communicate with you about your booking, stay, or enquiry.
  • Respond to questions submitted via our contact form or AI Assistant.
  • Improve our website, services, and the accuracy of our AI Assistant.
  • Maintain the security of our property and guests.
  • Comply with legal, regulatory, or accounting obligations.

3. Our AI Assistant

Our website includes an AI Assistant that can answer questions, help you submit a booking, and schedule viewing appointments on your behalf. When you interact with the AI Assistant:

  • Your messages and any details you provide (such as name, contact details, dates, and apartment preferences) are processed in order to carry out your request.
  • Information gathered by the AI Assistant for a booking or appointment is handled the same way as information submitted through our standard forms.
  • Conversations may be reviewed by our team to confirm bookings, resolve issues, or improve the assistant's accuracy.
  • You should avoid sharing sensitive information (such as full payment card numbers) directly in the chat window.

4. Cookies & Similar Technologies

We may use cookies or similar technologies to remember your preferences, understand how visitors use our website, and improve performance. You can disable cookies through your browser settings; however, some features of the website, including booking forms, may not function correctly without them.

5. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Staff and management of Prime Court Apartments, for the purpose of fulfilling your booking or enquiry.
  • Service providers who support our booking system, payment processing, or AI Assistant functionality, under appropriate confidentiality obligations.
  • Authorities, where required by law, regulation, or legal process.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy, including any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements, after which it is securely deleted or anonymised.

8. Your Rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal or contractual obligations.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 11 below.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for adults capable of entering into a booking agreement. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
